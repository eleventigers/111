Cloud

I used to operate rails of the sky. I was part of an endless network, built long long time ago. I covered some quiet and distant areas where no one else were brave to go. Sky was fresh and easy to deal with, so I moved fluently. My brother Wind supplied me with energy, in return I built him a fragrant playground.


Although my rails were quite busy, transporting droplets, flakes and all kinds of waters, there were birds, hopping on and off, travelling great lengths, resting their wings on my puffy pillows...


When people got up to the sky, I had to hide. I couldn't stand their enthusiasm and arrogance. They were littering all the corners and smoking all over the place. Brother Wind got ill, upset and angry. My rails became quite a mess. I lost control.


I couldn't believe how quickly everything changed. I knew that humans had something special so I had to come down and look around. It wasn't really smart decision but I must say that I got very into studying human nature.


Down on the ground, I became inspired and started to collect all sorts of sounds that could be weaved into my mind rails. I am now an artist and you may listen to my song. Does it sound like me?

The day before, I had called a UFO hotline in the yellow pages to report a UFO sighting I had made whilst stood in the swimming pool area of the apartment complex. I wished I hadn't called cos from then on, I was paranoid that I was being "watched" by the FBI. Anyway, back to the hummingbird. A small fly flew towards my face and I instinctively batted it away. Unfortunately, I batted it in the direction of the Hummingbird, who, not really expecting a meal, simply parted it's beak and the poor fly disappeared into it's small gob. Dead FOREVER.

Ocean

My mind stretches, bends and breathes over deep and shallow valleys. It goes in all directions and reaches far away corners. I am quite sure that you love to read it sometimes. After all, you came off my mind. I know, it can be pretty hectic sometimes but what do you expect from a head that is full of fish?


They are my thoughts, constantly moving, staging battles and appropriating friendships. Their colors and shapes play silly games, I never get bored being around them. And those little creatures that crawl my mind cracks are working hard to keep them clean.


I am not sure where I began but I recall that life was a dream. I was instructed to stream it to earth where reality was a long time queen. Dream that I lived in hid a tremendous meaning therefore it made earth feel great to let everything in. Dream overthrown reality and I began building my first mind structures. It took me ages to get them working though…


If only the world understood what the dream was for. I am going insane deeper and deeper, where fish die and I conduct this absurd choir of spirits. No one has heard their songs before. They’re naked reflections of my disturbed mind so you might feel awkward listening to them. If you insist, please come, hear the distant dream :

Night

At the center of an infinitely old and vast as Ocean forest, lies an ancient place. Trees guard it peacefully, breathing in dense mist that appears to be a living curtain. Those who live nearby know that the mist hides a mysterious forest theatre. Its stage is a … ginger lake that no one dears to swim in.


When evening turns to night, animals rush to get the best possible seats made of a warm moss and fallen leaves. Air is full of whisper and excitement, animals are guessing the imminent performance. Even though the play is as old as the universe everyone seems to love watching it again and again, keeping the forest alive.


Suddenly, ginger lake trembles and a little wave wets a finger of a napping brown bear. He falls down and rotates in horror unconcerned about anyone’s safety… Forest laughs and brings him back to the seat. Bear now knows that lake does not bite.


Lake trembles once again and the mist rains down in seconds, blending with the drones of forest crickets. Deafening silence follows and all the forest dwellers freeze looking to where I come from. I slowly emerge from water spreading the night all over the forest…. What happens next knows only the audience but you may listen to the raw sonic recording :

Wind

Oh, little Cloud, she is so naive and childish. She wants me to play in this beautiful meadow but I am just too busy working around the town. Once a master of perfumes, king of the big Valley, I am a waste collector now. Long gone are the days when I breezed past flowers flirting my way through life.


Even though, I am an arrogant and pretentious character sometimes, I can make fragrance that is as Divine as Night… However, the world has changed and it’s pretty gloomy so I had to come down and adapt, become a trashy city dweller that recycles deadly aromas.


Do not think that I despise bin men – I actually love them. They helped me to apply my mixing skills to all sorts of objects. They showed me where beauty lies in the rubbish. I discovered loads of new ways or combining the pleasant with the unpleasant.


There are loads of kids digging stuff in the city’s wasteland. They quickly became my finest friend, letting me fly their glorious trash kites… Those kids know where to find raw and fresh sounds so I got to listen to the most innovative music that I had never heard of.


I began aggregating and remixing all those little sonic pleasure into what I believe reflects my windy being best, have a listen :

My cousin once told me that whatever direction you are pointing in Edinburgh, Scotland the wind will blow directly in your face. I didn't believe him until one day, I had to cycle all over Edinburgh delivering pies. It is true.

Love

I searched for a dream all around, inside and outside, wide and close but I could only bearly smell it. That dream where you and me run through a forest naked, chasing light while drinking up mist. We fly dancing, collecting sun rays into a giant shining ball.


The light absorbs us as we take the ball up to an ancient temple. We melt into each other as we struggle climbing infinite steps. We become one… We call it Love.


This dream is real and is happening right now. You made me realize what I could never grasp. I was missing a part so there was no way to continue up the steps. There was no way to see what’s up there, on top.


I missed you all my life. Without you that dream would have killed me eventually. You are more than a dream. You are Love.


There is nothing more certain than you, Love. You make dreams and reality melt into each other. It is an absolute happiness.
